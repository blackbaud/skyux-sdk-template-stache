Whether you have a Support, Contact Us, or an About page, you should provide a way for people to know who created the site and how to contact you with questions. You may even want to explain what your team is responsible for and what your user is responsible for.

## Contact Us We recommend you provide a preferred way to contact your team, such as a Slack channel or team email address. ## Reporting Issues How do you want people to report issues or ask for help? ## Error Messages Are there common error messages you can explain to your users?
